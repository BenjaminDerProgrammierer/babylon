The Night Shift at Moonlit Zoo

When the gates close and the visitors go home, Keeper Nia walks the lantern-lit paths to check on the animals who only become themselves after midnight.

Chapter 1: Clucky the Gate Scout

The smallest resident of Moonlit Zoo spots everything first, including the rusty side gate that never quite stays shut.

Chapter 2: Bramble of the Hay Barn

The horse is the zoo's midnight shuttle, trotting softly between the stables, the orchard, and the moonflower patch behind the aviary.

Chapter 3: Rufus, the Lantern Hound

Every evening he patrols the walkways, tail high and nose working, making sure no snack cart, squirrel, or escaped balloon goes unnoticed.

Chapter 4: Pippa from the Ice House

The penguin keeps the coolest corner of the zoo in order and insists on sliding through puddles whenever the moonlight hits the tiles.

Chapter 5: Miso, Chief of Mischief

The kitten lives in the ticket booth, steals the keeper's keys, and naps on the map that leads to the hidden greenhouse.
